A two-phase exploration of personal companion robots, starting from a Disney-inspired droid and ending with a deliberate decision to discontinue.

**Phase one: droid robots, 2023.** Personal companion robots for the home: a Pokémon for everyone. Vision, navigation, and audio capabilities. Built and shipped a working prototype. Targeted at companion-to-children, support-for-elderly, and home-manager use cases. Raised $250K pre-seed on the back of the prototype and the early discovery work.

**Phase two: companion + chore variant, 2024.** Asked a sharper question: would utility and character compound? Explored combining the companion personality with a chore-doing robot vacuum. Ran user research with 100+ current robot vacuum owners: workflows, pain points, willingness to pay.

<ImageFigure src={companionChore} alt="Companion + Chore Robot concept render" caption="Phase two: chore-doing variant concept" />

**The kill decision.** The market preferred a quiet, reliable utility over a personable one; the premium for companionship did not justify the additional cost or compromise in cleaning performance. Synthesized the research into a deliberate decision to discontinue rather than push through.
