Infinite-canvas, node-based AI tool for product visualization. Collapses CAD, Blender, and image generation models into a single workflow. Built for hardware companies, DTC brands, and growth teams that need consistent product visuals across platforms and variations.

**Design.** Designed the node-based canvas interface and the AI agent that compiles natural-language intent into workflow graphs.

**Agent orchestration.** Built planner/executor split, retrieval layer for conditional context loading, and a structured eval harness.

**Infrastructure.** Built on fal.ai: managed cold starts, shared serverless pools, and async submit-and-poll patterns.

**Go-to-market.** Ran GTM across a LinkedIn beta launch, Reddit (r/IndustrialDesign, r/SideProject, r/blender), and a 200-person outbound list.

**Customer discovery.** Parallel customer discovery across two ICPs: growth marketers and industrial designers.
